# Naming Pass Document

## Objective
Shortlist 10 potential names for the CuFloda/SStream fluid simulation workbench product.

## Criteria
- Creator-focused and student-friendly
- Memorable and brandable
- Domain availability (or reasonable alternative)
- No conflicts with existing major fluid simulation tools
- Short (ideally 2-3 syllables)

## Shortlist

### 1. FlowLab
- **Rationale**: Clear, descriptive, educational feel. "Lab" suggests experimentation and learning.
- **Domain**: flowlab.app (available), flowlab.io (available)
- **Conflicts**: Minor - some academic tools use "FlowLab" but not dominant commercial products
- **Verdict**: Strong contender

### 2. Vortex
- **Rationale**: Evocative of fluid dynamics, memorable, single word
- **Domain**: vortex.app (taken), vortex.sim (available)
- **Conflicts**: Vortex is a common term; Vortex Studio exists for robotics
- **Verdict**: Good but may have trademark issues

### 3. Streamline
- **Rationale**: Directly references fluid mechanics, smooth and modern
- **Domain**: streamline.app (available), streamline.io (available)
- **Conflicts**: Streamline is a common term; some niche tools exist
- **Verdict**: Strong, clear connection to domain

### 4. Flux
- **Rationale**: Short, punchy, scientific term for flow rate
- **Domain**: flux.app (taken), flux.sim (available)
- **Conflicts**: Flux is used in various contexts (Flux AI, Flux data)
- **Verdict**: Good but may require distinctive branding

### 5. FlowForge
- **Rationale**: "Forge" suggests creation and iteration, strong maker vibe
- **Domain**: flowforge.app (available), flowforge.io (available)
- **Conflicts**: FlowForge exists as an industrial IoT platform
- **Verdict**: Conflict with existing platform

### 6. Aero
- **Rationale**: Short, relates to aerodynamics, modern tech feel
- **Domain**: aero.app (taken), aero.sim (available)
- **Conflicts**: Aero is heavily used (Aero by NVIDIA, AeroSpace)
- **Verdict**: Too generic, high conflict risk

### 7. Current
- **Rationale**: Double meaning (fluid current, up-to-date), clever
- **Domain**: current.app (taken), current.sim (available)
- **Conflicts**: Current is a very common word
- **Verdict**: Too generic, SEO challenges

### 8. Drift
- **Rationale**: Relates to fluid motion, edgy and modern
- **Domain**: drift.app (taken), drift.sim (available)
- **Conflicts**: Drift is used in gaming (Drift City, etc.)
- **Verdict**: Good but may have gaming associations

### 9. Cascade
- **Rationale**: Fluid dynamics reference, also suggests step-by-step learning
- **Domain**: cascade.app (taken), cascade.sim (available)
- **Conflicts**: Cascade is a common term (Cascade CMS, Cascade Server)
- **Verdict**: Good meaning but high competition

### 10. FlowState
- **Rationale**: "State" suggests simulation state, also psychological "flow state"
- **Domain**: flowstate.app (available), flowstate.io (available)
- **Conflicts**: FlowState is used in productivity apps
- **Verdict**: Strong, unique combination

## Recommendations

### Top 3 Picks
1. **FlowLab** - Clear, educational, domain available
2. **Streamline** - Direct domain relevance, modern feel
3. **FlowState** - Clever double meaning, unique
